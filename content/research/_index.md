The reSAID Lab builds the methods, tools, and empirical understanding needed to
engineer AI-enabled software that is reliable, fair, safe, and maintainable. Our
work spans the full lifecycle of AI software — from how large language models
reason and generate code, to how ML components are specified, verified, tested,
and maintained in real systems. The themes below describe the questions we focus
on; the projects that follow show how we pursue them, and each links to the
[publications](/publications/) it produced.
